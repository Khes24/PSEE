Philippine Society for Engineering Education (PSEE) is a duly registered non-profit organization in the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) of the Republic of the Philippines with SEC Registration No. 2021080022321-21.

Vision, Mission, Values, Objectives

Our Vision

To be the catalyst for faculty development in engineering in the Philippines in the areas of instruction, research, and extension with global relevance.

Our Mission

Contribute to nation-building by advancing research, innovation, and excellence in engineering education.

Our Values

Passion, Commitment, Excellence, Innovation, Integrity, Collaboration, Respect.

Our Objectives

PSEE aims to contribute to the improvement of the quality, relevance, and implementation of engineering education in the Philippines. The objectives of the society are to:

  1. Serve as the focal point for information on all aspects of engineering education for engineering educators and practitioners in the Philippines
  2. Contribute to the innovation and excellence in learning and teaching by promoting the development and use of new teaching and learning techniques and tools and assessment and evaluation of learning effectiveness in engineering
  3. Promote continuous and lifelong learning, innovation, and transformation of every engineering educato
  4. Provide coaching and mentorship to new engineering educators
  5. Promote a research culture and scholarship within the society to reposition engineering educators to commit to scholarly work and research output
  6. Organize conferences, seminars, symposia, and webinars to provide avenues for engineering educators and industry practitioners to present applied or scholarly research output
  7. Provide avenues for continuous interaction between academic and industrypractitioners to bridge the gap between what academe offers and what industry needs
  8. Identify, quantify and make more visible the increasing needs for specific advanced engineering skills in the Philippines
  9. Develop local and global linkages with similarly minded organizations and institutions
